Oreo Truffles

Description:

Oreos are a brand of sandwich cookie consisting of two wafers with a sweet creme filling. A chocolate truffle is a type of chocolate confectionary, traditionally made with a chocolate ganache, usually in a spherical shape.

By following this simple recipe, you will learn how to make delicious cake pops at home with inexpensive ingredients. A fun snack for yourself, your children, and even house guests!

Ingredients:

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages of semisweet baking chocolate
  • 16 ounces of cream-filled chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo), divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate in a saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally, 4 to 6 minutes.
  2. While the chocolate is melting, place 9 cream-filled sandwich cookies in a food processor and blend until finely crushed. Transfer crumbs to a bowl and reserve for decorating.
  3. Add remaining cookies to the food processor and blend until finely crushed. Transfer crumbs to a large bowl and stir in cream cheese; mix until well-blended. Roll mixture into 42 balls, about 1-inch in diameter.
  4. Place each ball in melted chocolate and use two forks to roll it until fully coated. Life the ball with both forks and allow excess chocolate to drip off. Place coated truffle on a wax paper-lined baking sheet and sprinkle with reserved cookie crumbs to decorate. Repeat to make remaining truffles.
  5. Refrigerate truffles until firm, at least 1 hour. Keep in refrigerator until just before serving.
